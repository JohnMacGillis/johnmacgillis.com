Dominion · the game

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Dominion is a single-player game. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

What the app stores

Your game progress — the story beat you're on, your reporter's byline, your choices and rank, and your sound and motion preferences — is saved only on your own device. None of it leaves your phone, and we cannot see it.

What we don't do

  • We don't require an account or a login.
  • We don't collect names, email addresses, contacts, or location.
  • We don't use third-party advertising or tracking.
  • We don't run analytics that identify you.

Crash reports

If your device is set to share diagnostics with developers, Apple may provide us with anonymous, aggregated crash information through TestFlight and the App Store to help us fix bugs. This never includes personal data, and it is handled by Apple, not collected by us.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.
